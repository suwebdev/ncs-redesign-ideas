Information Sessions
Happen Every Month

Flexible. Affordable. High quality.

Hybrid courses facilitated by world-class instructors allow you to make the most of online and face-to-face instruction.

Digital Technology & Cultures

Explore what it means to be human in the digital age. Critique software, video games, websites, and social media platforms. Analyze digital technology’s role in the creation of 21st-century culture and society.

Organizational Leadership

Become an effective and ethical leader. Develop a leadership philosophy based upon leadership and organizational theory, practical experience, and personal reflection.

Web Development Certificate

Learn to build world-class websites and applications using cutting-edge technologies including Javascript, HTML5 and CSS3 to bring content alive.
